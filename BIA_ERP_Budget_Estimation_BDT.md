# 7. VAT, Tax and Statutory Treatment

All figures in this annex are **exclusive of VAT, withholding/advance income tax and any other statutory deduction**. The proposal should state:

- Applicable VAT and taxes will be added or deducted according to Bangladesh law and the vendor’s legal/tax status at invoice date.
- The client will issue all legally required withholding certificates within the statutory period.
- The contract value is the vendor’s commercial price before client-side withholding; withholding must not be treated as a price discount.
- Any change in tax law or rate after bid validity will be handled according to applicable law.

The final VAT/tax wording should be reviewed by a Bangladesh-qualified tax adviser before proposal submission.

# 8. Exchange-Rate Protection

For a long implementation, use one of these mechanisms:

1. **Preferred:** Contract directly in BDT at **৳6.138 crore**, with no FX adjustment unless imported licences/cloud services change.
2. Contract in USD with BDT invoices calculated using the agreed bank selling/reference rate on invoice date.
3. Fix BDT for 90 days, then adjust only if USD/BDT moves more than ±3% from the base rate of 124.00.

Suggested clause:

> The commercial estimate uses USD 1 = BDT 124.00. If the published reference or agreed bank selling rate changes by more than three percent before an unpaid future milestone becomes due, the foreign-currency-dependent portion of that milestone may be adjusted proportionately by mutual written confirmation.

# 10. Final BDT Bid Position

## Recommended proposal headline

**Total fixed price: BDT 6,13,80,000 excluding VAT and tax**  
**Delivery period: 12-14 months**  
**Total effort: 1,709 man-days**  
**Core team: 8-10 professionals**  
**Warranty/hypercare: 30 days after production go-live, with the detailed defect warranty period stated in contract**

## Negotiation floor

Do not reduce the complete production-ready scope below approximately **৳4.65 crore** without removing modules, integrations, report volume, migration cycles, assurance or delivery capacity. A price near **৳3.75 crore** should be positioned as Phase 1 only—not as the entire enterprise ERP.

## Recommended negotiation sequence

1. Present **৳6.138 crore** as the fully controlled baseline.
2. Offer milestone payments and phased go-live before offering a discount.
3. If budget pressure remains, move modules to Phase 2 instead of reducing engineering quality.
4. Make the fixed price conditional on signed SRS, assumptions, exclusions and capped integrations.
5. Quote all post-baseline changes at the agreed BDT rate and add schedule impact.
