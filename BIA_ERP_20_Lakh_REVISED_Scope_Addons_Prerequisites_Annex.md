1. BIA submits a written add-on request referencing the add-on ID.

5. Add-on acceptance and warranty are governed by its own quotation/change order.

# 5. Delivery Protection Rules

1. The 8–10 month schedule begins after PRE-01, PRE-02 and PRE-37 are complete.
2. A milestone schedule is confirmed only when its required prerequisites are available.
3. BIA delay in decisions, data, examples, environment, UAT or payment extends the delivery date by at least the delay period.
4. Delay exceeding 15 calendar days allows resource rescheduling and issue of a revised plan.
5. Only one consolidated feedback list will be processed per milestone review cycle.
6. Add-on requests do not block acceptance of completed base deliverables.
7. A manual import/entry feature is the base acceptance method where an external integration is listed as an add-on.
8. The base report limit is 15 definitions; every different material layout/calculation is separately counted.
9. Source-data cleansing and statutory/legal interpretation remain BIA responsibilities.
10. Source-code handover occurs after full contractual payment.
