# 1. Bangladesh Market View

The earlier BDT annex converted international software rates into taka. That is useful for global comparison but is not the best basis for a competitive proposal to a Bangladeshi organization. This budget is rebuilt from local salary levels, local operating costs, a lean Bangladesh team, local client expectations and typical tender risks.

BIA's three-page requirement is larger than an ordinary HR/payroll package. It includes:

- HR and employee records;
- leave and biometric attendance integration;
- payroll, PF, gratuity and salary advances;
- accounts, receipts, payments, funds and petty cash;
- inventory and employee/item assignment;
- member billing, online payment and portal;
- regulatory file/document management;
- SMS, email, reports, backup and security.

Low-cost Bangladeshi ERP packages advertised for ৳2-8 lakh are normally prebuilt SME products with limited customization. They are not a suitable benchmark for a bespoke, audited, integrated ERP with payroll, accounting, payment, biometric devices, portals and migration.

As a stronger market reference, a Bangladesh public procurement notice for an HR and Accounts ERP required prior similar software-development experience with a minimum contract value of **৳1.5 crore**, even though that procurement did not contain all the BIA modules in this scope ([Bangladesh Public Procurement Authority notice](https://www.bppa.gov.bd/advertisement-works/details-81171.html)). This supports a crore-level—not a few-lakh—budget for a credible enterprise implementation.

# 2. Recommended Bangladesh Pricing Options

| Package | Fixed price | Timeline | Recommended use |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Option A — Core Automation | **৳85 lakh** | 8-10 months | Budget-constrained first phase only |
| **Option B — Recommended BIA ERP** | **৳1.95 crore** | **12-15 months** | Full practical production scope |
| Option C — Enterprise Plus | **৳2.75 crore** | 15-18 months | Higher resilience, deeper finance and extended assurance |

## Option A — Core Automation: ৳85 lakh

Includes simplified employee profile, leave, one attendance device, core payroll, basic receipts/payments, basic inventory, basic member records and standard reports.

It should exclude or limit:

- full double-entry accounting and advanced financial statements;
- advanced PF/gratuity/tax rules;
- member online payment and reconciliation;
- full document routing/version/retention;
- extensive workflow configuration;
- large historical migration;
- advanced dashboards and report customization;
- HA/DR and independent penetration testing.

This is not the recommended final enterprise solution. It is a phased entry option.

## Option B — Recommended BIA ERP: ৳1.95 crore

Includes the practical production-ready scope described in the main estimation report:

- complete employee records and HR history;
- configurable leave and approval workflow;
- attendance device, shifts, exception correction and overtime;
- payroll, advances, PF, gratuity, payslip and bank/cash outputs;
- controlled double-entry accounts, voucher, ledger and financial reports;
- inventory requisition, receipt, issue, assignment, return and stock ledger;
- member invoice, collection, payment integration, receipt and portal;
- BIA file registry, documents, movement, versioning and reminders;
- RBAC, maker-checker, audit trail and 2FA;
- SMS, email, dashboard, PDF/Excel reports and backup;
- two migration rehearsals, two payroll parallel runs, training and go-live support.

## Option C — Enterprise Plus: ৳2.75 crore

Adds selected capabilities such as procurement, supplier/AP workflow, fixed assets and depreciation, advanced budgeting, broader tax automation, multiple device/bank/payment integrations, high availability, stronger DR, extended performance testing, independent security assessment and longer on-site support.

The software estimate remains approximately **1,709 man-days** at proposal stage. Bangladesh pricing is possible through a blended local team rather than applying a foreign hourly rate to every man-day.

# 8. VAT, Tax and Tender Presentation

Present the price as:

> **Total software development and implementation price: BDT 1,95,00,000 excluding applicable VAT and taxes.**

Do not write “including all taxes” unless a Bangladesh tax professional has calculated the vendor-specific impact. VAT, withholding tax and income-tax treatment can depend on the vendor’s legal status, service classification, registration and the client’s procurement rules.

The proposal should clarify:

- quoted price is before applicable VAT and client withholding;
- the client will provide statutory deduction certificates;
- hardware and recurring third-party charges are excluded;
- proposal validity is 30 days;
- pricing assumes work mainly from the vendor’s Dhaka office;
- travel and accommodation outside Dhaka are reimbursable if requested.

# 11. Exclusions and Change-Control Items

- Native Android/iOS apps and offline mode.
- Additional organizations, branches with materially different policies or multi-tenant SaaS.
- Procurement/tendering, complete fixed assets or complex tax automation unless explicitly added.
- More device brands/sites, banks, gateways, SMS providers or legacy-system integrations.
- Attendance hardware, networking, biometric SDK licence and device-vendor charges.
- Historical data cleansing, scanning, manual data entry and OCR.
- Commercial licences, cloud bills, SMS, email and payment transaction fees.
- Independent penetration-testing vendor and formal compliance certification.
- 24×7 support, permanent on-site team and disaster-recovery data centre.
- Requirements not documented in the signed SRS or approved change requests.

All additions should be estimated at **৳1,750/hour blended** or separately fixed after impact analysis.

# 12. Negotiation Strategy for a Bangladeshi Client

## Opening position

Submit **৳2.15 crore** as the initial commercial offer if negotiation is expected. Keep authority to settle near **৳1.95 crore** without reducing the recommended baseline.

## Target and floor

| Position | Amount | Treatment |
|---|---:|---|
| Initial proposal | **৳2.15 crore** | Allows normal negotiation and report/integration clarification |
| Target closing price | **৳1.95 crore** | Recommended full baseline |
| Conditional strategic price | **৳1.75 crore** | Only with advance payment, quick decisions and tightly capped scope |
| Absolute full-scope floor | **৳1.60 crore** | High delivery risk; remove features or warranty/support below this point |
| Phase 1 alternative | **৳85 lakh** | Reduced scope; never label it full ERP |

## What to trade instead of giving a cash discount

- Reduce report count and custom formats.
- Move advanced Accounts, Member Portal or File workflow to Phase 2.
- Limit migration to opening balances and one year of history.
- Limit device/payment/bank integrations to one each.
- Reduce on-site days and use train-the-trainer delivery.
- Request 20% advance or faster milestone payment.
- Convert optional features to time-and-material enhancements.

Never accept an undefined phrase such as “reports and more” inside a fixed price. Replace it with a numbered report catalogue and signed assumptions.
