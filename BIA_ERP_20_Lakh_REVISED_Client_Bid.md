# 1. Executive Proposal

This revised proposal offers a controlled basic ERP within BIA's available budget of BDT 20,00,000. The base implementation covers essential functionality in every named business area while limiting complex workflows, integrations, reports and statutory automation.

The base system is intended to establish usable digital operations and reliable master data. Advanced capabilities will be implemented only as separately approved add-ons after the base system is accepted and the relevant requirements are confirmed.

# 2. Base Delivery Principles

- Every named module is represented at an essential operational level.
- Standardized screens and workflows will be used to control cost and delivery time.
- Manual import/entry is the base method where external integration is not included.
- Client-approved formulas and samples will control attendance, payroll and accounts output.
- The base report catalogue is limited to 15 signed-off reports.
- Changes after SRS approval require separate price and schedule approval.
- Add-ons do not form part of the BDT 20,00,000 commitment.

# 4. Included Base Scope

## 4.1 Platform and Administration

- One BIA organization and one policy set.
- Secure login, logout, password change and administrator reset.
- Basic user activation/deactivation.
- Predefined Admin, HR, Approver, Payroll, Accounts, Store, Membership, File Officer and Employee roles.
- Basic module and action permissions.
- Department, designation, employee category, holiday and general settings.
- Basic activity history for selected sensitive actions.
- Basic database backup configuration in the agreed environment.

## 4.2 HRM and Employee Profile

- Employee code, name, contact, address and basic identification.
- Marital, dependant, nominee and emergency-contact information.
- Joining date, department, designation, category, status and reporting manager.
- Basic bank information, photograph and selected document upload.
- Basic transfer, increment and separation history.
- Employee list, search, Excel import and export.
- Employee self-service view of own profile, leave, attendance and payslip.

## 4.3 Leave Management

- Leave types and annual quota.
- Opening leave balance import.
- Full-day and half-day online application.
- Available-balance and date-overlap validation.
- One-step manager approval/rejection.
- Basic cancellation before payroll lock under the agreed rule.
- Leave balance, history and two standard reports.
- Submission and decision email through one client-provided email service.

## 4.4 Attendance Management

- Excel/CSV attendance-log import using one approved format.
- Employee attendance-ID mapping.
- Basic office start/end time and late grace.
- First-in/last-out processing.
- Present, absent, leave, holiday, late, early-exit and worked-hour results.
- Missing-punch identification.
- Basic overtime input/calculation using one approved rule.
- Authorized correction with reason and activity history.
- Daily, monthly and exception reports.

Direct attendance-device connection is not included in the base price; it is an add-on after a technical feasibility test.

## 4.5 Basic Payroll, PF, Gratuity and Advance

- One monthly payroll group.
- Basic salary scale and employee salary assignment.
- Maximum eight active earning/deduction components in total.
- One attendance-deduction formula.
- One overtime-payment formula.
- Basic PF contribution calculation using one approved formula.
- Basic gratuity informational calculation using one approved formula.
- Salary-advance amount, installment deduction and balance.
- Monthly variable input for agreed bonus/adjustment.
- Gross salary, deduction, net salary and one rounding rule.
- Payroll preview, exception list, one-step approval and lock.
- Payroll register, department summary and PDF payslip.
- One bank salary sheet in Excel plus basic cash/cheque lists.
- Payslip access from employee self-service.

Automated tax, PF interest/settlement, gratuity provisioning/final settlement and retroactive payroll are add-ons.

## 4.6 Basic Accounts Registers

- Income and expense heads/sub-heads.
- Basic cash and bank account setup.
- Receipt and payment entry with automatic numbering.
- Printable money receipt.
- Basic fund and petty-cash records.
- Draft and one-step finalization.
- Protection from normal edit/delete after finalization.
- Cash register, bank register and receipt/payment register.
- Basic account-wise transaction ledger.
- Income and expenditure summary.
- Payroll-payment reference/summary entry.

The base module is an operational receipt/payment and cashbook system. Full double-entry accounting, journal voucher, trial balance, balance sheet, AP/AR, budgeting, reconciliation and statutory accounting are separate add-ons.

## 4.7 Basic Inventory and Assignment

- Item category, unit, item and store setup.
- Opening quantity import.
- Product requisition and one-step approval.
- Goods receipt and stock issue.
- Employee/department assignment.
- Product return.
- Basic stock adjustment with reason.
- Negative-quantity prevention.
- Item, current-stock, requisition and assignment reports.

Procurement, purchase order, costing/valuation, supplier bills, batch, serial, warranty, expiry and barcode are add-ons.

## 4.8 Member Records, Billing and Basic Portal

- Member category, unique number, name, contact, status and basic documents.
- Member list, search and Excel import.
- Basic fee/charge setup.
- Opening due, invoice generation and printable invoice.
- Manual payment entry, invoice allocation and printable receipt.
- Outstanding balance and collection reports.
- One portal login linked to one member.
- Read-only portal access to own profile, invoices, payments and receipts.
- Selected invoice/payment email notification.

Online payment gateway, automated recurring billing, penalties, waivers, refunds and multiple representatives are add-ons.

## 4.9 Basic File and Document Register

- IDRA, Ministry, Sadharan Bima Corporation, NBR, ICC and FBCCI categories.
- File/register number, subject, reference, date, responsible officer and status.
- Basic inward/outward correspondence information.
- Supporting-document upload.
- Current pending-with officer and due date.
- Open, pending and closed status.
- File search, register and pending/overdue report.

Movement history, multi-step routing, document versioning, OCR, retention, legal hold and advanced reminders are add-ons.

## 4.10 Notifications, Dashboards and Reports

- One client-provided email/SMTP service.
- Basic leave, payslip, member and file email events as agreed.
- Basic HR, attendance, payroll, accounts, inventory and membership summary cards.
- Maximum 15 signed-off standard report definitions across all modules.
- PDF/print or Excel/CSV output only where specified in the approved report catalogue.
- Role-based access to included reports.

SMS, scheduled reports, custom report builder and advanced analytics are add-ons.

## 4.11 Data Migration

- Approved templates for employee, leave balance, basic account opening, stock and member opening data.
- Basic required-field and master-reference validation.
- One trial import in UAT.
- One corrected final opening-data import.
- Record-count and agreed control-total reconciliation.

Historical transaction migration, cleansing, duplicate investigation, scanning and manual data entry are excluded/add-ons.

# 8. Mandatory Prerequisites for Timely Delivery

The delivery schedule is valid only when the following prerequisites are satisfied.

## 8.1 Governance and Decisions

- BIA will appoint one authorized Product Owner/Project Coordinator before kickoff.
- BIA will nominate one responsible representative for HR, Payroll, Accounts, Inventory, Membership and File Management.
- BIA will provide one consolidated decision or issue list for each submission.
- Document, prototype and UAT decisions will be provided within five business days.
- Conflicting instructions from different stakeholders will be resolved by BIA's Product Owner.
- New requirements will not be inserted into an active milestone without approved change control.

## 8.2 Rules and Samples

- Leave, attendance, overtime, payroll, PF, gratuity and advance rules must be supplied before the related design begins.
- Every calculation must include client-approved worked examples and expected totals.
- Accounts heads, opening values, receipt/payment formats and income/expenditure mapping must be supplied before Accounts development.
- BIA must approve the final list and format of 15 reports during SRS.
- BIA is responsible for legal, statutory, tax and accounting interpretation.

## 8.3 Data Readiness

- BIA will provide clean data only in the agreed templates.
- Mandatory values and unique employee/member codes must be completed before import.
- BIA will remove duplicates and correct invalid source data.
- Trial files must be delivered by the agreed milestone date.
- Corrected final files must be delivered at least five business days before final migration.
- Authorized module representatives must sign record counts and financial opening totals.

## 8.4 Infrastructure and Access

- BIA will provide the required server/cloud, domain, SSL certificate and administrative access on schedule.
- BIA will provide a working email/SMTP service and sufficient sending permission.
- BIA will provide test and production user details.
- Firewall, VPN, network and provider approvals are BIA responsibilities.
- Hardware, cloud, domain, certificate and provider charges are paid by BIA.

## 8.5 UAT and Acceptance

- BIA will nominate trained UAT users for each module.
- UAT feedback must be one consolidated written list, not separate informal messages.
- BIA will complete review within seven business days of each milestone release.
- BIA will distinguish defects from enhancements/new requirements.
- BIA will make staff available for payroll and opening-balance reconciliation.
- Milestones that pass agreed acceptance cases will be signed without waiting for unrelated future add-ons.

## 8.6 Commercial Prerequisites

- Mobilization begins after contract signature and 20% advance payment.
- Milestone invoices are payable within 10 calendar days.
- Applicable VAT/tax and client withholding are outside the BDT 20,00,000 base price.
- BIA will provide all legally required withholding certificates.
- Delayed payment may pause work and extend the delivery schedule.

# 9. Schedule-Relief Clause

If BIA delays a required decision, data file, formula, sample, infrastructure access, UAT response or payment, the delivery date will move by at least the resulting delay period. If the delay exceeds 15 calendar days, assigned resources may be rescheduled and a revised delivery plan will be issued. Such delay is not a supplier default.

# 10. Acceptance and Change Control

- The signed SRS, base feature list, report catalogue and approved examples form the scope baseline.
- BIA will accept each milestone or provide one consolidated list of material in-scope defects within seven business days.
- A defect is an approved base requirement that fails to operate as documented.
- A new field group, formula, workflow, role, integration, report or changed policy is an enhancement/change request.
- Add-ons require separate written quotation and authorization.
- Minor non-blocking issues may be scheduled for an agreed patch without withholding acceptance of unrelated completed work.

# 11. Exclusions

- Every add-on listed in Section 6 until separately contracted.
- Native Android/iOS applications and offline operation.
- Full Bangla interface or translation.
- Data cleansing, scanning, OCR and manual data entry.
- Additional entities, sites, devices, banks, providers or gateways.
- Third-party API, hardware, cloud, SMS, email and transaction charges.
- Formal compliance certification, independent penetration testing and 24×7 support.
- Features not listed in the signed revised base scope.

# 12. Warranty and Handover

- A 45-calendar-day defect warranty begins after production acceptance.
- Warranty covers reproducible defects in the signed base scope.
- Changes in policy, law, third-party service or client data are not warranty defects.
- Source code and agreed technical notes will be handed over after full payment.
- Maintenance, support and future add-ons will be covered by a separate agreement.

# 13. Commercial Validity

- This offer is valid for 30 calendar days from the proposal date.
- The fixed price depends on the scope limits and prerequisites in this document.
- Any conflicting tender condition or requirement disclosed after contract signature is subject to impact assessment.
